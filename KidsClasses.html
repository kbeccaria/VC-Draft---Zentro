Menu

Class menu includes a main dish, a side dish and a dessert. Each class takes about two and a half hours depending on the menu and party size. The price is $58 per person. Minimum of 6, maximum of 15 students.
If an adult wishes watch, there is an additional fee of $25 per adult.

Main Dish Options

  • Parmesan Chicken Tenders
  • Pesto Pizza with chicken and pine nuts
  • Penne Pasta with garlic cream sauce
  • Potato Chip Chicken with honey mustard sauce
  • Chicken and Cheese Enchiladas
  • Gourmet Pigs in a Blanket with cheese and honey mustard sauce

Side Dish Options

  • Spring Salad with handmade croutons & dresing
  • Potato Stacks with herbs and parmesan cheese
  • Roasted Seasonal Vegetables
  • Couscous with peas and veggies
  • Wild Rice Medley
  • Garlic & Herb Mashed Potatos

Dessert Options

  • Molten Lava Cakes
  • Bananas Foster with pineapple, vanilla ice cream and a puff pastry
  • Brownie Cupcakes filled with cream cheese
  • Fruit Crisp with pear, apple and cranberry
  • Carrot Cake Cupcakes with cream cheese frosting
