Menu

Class menu includes a main dish, two side dishes and a dessert. The class takes 3 hours to complete and the price is per person.

Filet...............................$99

Roasted filet mignon tenderloin with Gorgonzola cream sauce, served with oven roasted carrots and corn, with garlic herb rustic Yukon gold mashed potatoes. Dessert options below.

Salmon............................$90

Herb Panko crusted salmon with tomato, red onion and caper relish served with a rice medley of brown, white, and wild rice with oven roasted fresh green beans. Dessert options below.

Chicken Piccata...............$82

Chicken Piccata with a fresh lemon, mushroom, and caper sauce, served with herb mashed potatoes and oven roasted fresh green beans. Dessert options below.

Mushroom Risotto.............................$79

Mushroom risotto with grilled zesty chicken goujonettes served with an iceberg wedge lettuce salad with tomatoes, bacon, and freshly made 'Society' blue cheese dressing. Dessert options below. *Can be made vegetarian as well.

Skirt Steak......................$89

Grilled skirt steak marinated in a ginger hoisin sauce, served with Yukon gold 'smashed' potatoes with truffle oil, sour cream and green onion, alongside a salad with Roquefort cheese, sliced pears, and a Champagne vinaigrette. Dessert options below.

Lemon Risotto.............................$79

Creamy lemon risotto with asparagus and roasted zesty prawns, served with a fresh spring mix salad with fresh seasonal berries, herb croutons and drizzled with a white balsamic vinaigrette. Dessert options below. *Can be made vegetarian as well.

Lemon Parmesan Chicken.............$82

Lemon and herb parmesan chicken with dill sauce, served with roasted herb potatoes with onion and garlic, along with fresh oven roasted carrots. Dessert options below.

Pork Tenderloin.............$90

Herb roasted pork tenderloin with Port wine reduction sauce, served with a creamy herb polenta with parmesan cheese and fresh roasted three color zucchini squash. Dessert options below.
