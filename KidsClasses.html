Menu

Class menu includes a main dish, a side dish and a dessert. Each class takes about 2 and a half hours depending on the menu and guest size. The price is $58 per person. Minimum of 6, maximum of 15 students.
If an adult wishes to stay and supervise, there is an additional fee of $25 per adult.
